Our Atelier

Crafted in darkness,
worn in light.

SABLE was born in a small Milanese atelier in 2018, with a singular obsession: garments that feel as deliberate as architecture. Every stitch is placed with intention. Every textile is sourced from mills that have operated for generations. We do not follow seasons — we set them.

Our pieces are designed to age with you, acquiring character through wear. We limit each run to ensure exclusivity is not a marketing claim, but a simple fact of our process.
